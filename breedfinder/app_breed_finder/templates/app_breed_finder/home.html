What is BreedFinder

Welcome to BreedFinder, your go-to destination for quickly identifying the most probable breeds of your beloved cat or dog ! With just a snap of a photo, our intuitive web application provides you with the 4 most likely breed matches along with detailed descriptions of each potential breed. We also provide a Breedex to discover breeds of cats and dogs.

Features

  • Probable Breed Identification : Upload a photo of your cat or dog and discover the 4 most probable breed matches.
  • Comprehensive Breedex : Explore our extensive Breedex index containing information on various cat and dog breeds.
  • Detailed Descriptions :Get insightful descriptions for each of the identified probable breeds, including characteristics, history, and more.
  • User-Friendly Interface : Our easy-to-use interface ensures a seamless experience for all users.
